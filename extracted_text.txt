What is Transfer Learning? 
Transfer learning is a machine learning technique where a model developed for one task is

point
 
for
 
a
 
model
 
on
 
a
 
second

It's

Why MobileNetV2? 
MobileNetV2 is specifically designed for mobile and embedded vision applications. It features:
