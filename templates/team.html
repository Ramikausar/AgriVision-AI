About PlantCare AI

PlantCare AI is an intelligent plant disease detection system developed as part of an AI & Machine Learning academic project at Ganpat University. It leverages the power of Transfer Learning with MobileNetV2 to accurately classify 38 distinct plant diseases from a simple leaf photograph — empowering farmers and gardeners with instant, AI-driven diagnostics.

🧠

Deep Learning Core

Built on MobileNetV2 pre-trained on ImageNet, fine-tuned on 87,000+ plant leaf images for high-accuracy inference.

🌿

38 Disease Classes

Covers diseases across Apple, Tomato, Grape, Corn, Potato, and many more crops — enabling broad agricultural utility.

⚡

Real-Time Detection

Flask-powered backend delivers disease predictions and care recommendations in under one second.

📊

95% Accuracy

Achieved through careful dataset augmentation, transfer learning, and dropout regularization techniques.

Transfer Learning: MobileNetV2

By utilizing MobileNetV2 trained initially on ImageNet, PlantCare AI avoids the massive computational costs of from-scratch convolution while maintaining extreme accuracy.

Neural Layer Breakdown

  • 1. Input Tensor (224, 224, 3)
  • 2. MobileNetV2 Base Frozen Feature Extractor
  • 3. GlobalAveragePooling2D Dimensionality Reduction
  • 4. Classifier Head Dropout (0.2) → Linear (1280 → 38)
  • 5. Softmax 38 Probability Distributions
